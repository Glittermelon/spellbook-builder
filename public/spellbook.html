D&D Spellbook Generator

Welcome to your personalized spellbook for D&D 5e! This page is intended to give players an easy way to collect their spells in one place, letting everyone avoid the mess that's individually screenshotting every spell your level 10 wizard has on hand.

Instructions: Search for spells by name, school, level, or class. They will appear in the library section. To move a spell card between the library and your spellbook, simply click it. This can all be done without an account; if you want to save your spellbook, create a login. Each login can hold an unlimited number of characters, each able to hold their own unique spellbook (including the border color of the spellbook and the character's class-specific spellcasting information.)

Good luck in your campaign, and may the dice roll ever in your favor!
